          A foundational pillar of the design system was its native support for both dark
          and light themes. This was a critical architectural decision, driven directly by
          the operational realities of a global hotel company. For hotel associates working
          at all hours of the day and night, the ability to switch between themes is
          essential for reducing eye strain and maintaining focus. By providing this
          flexibility, we ensured a comfortable, accessible, and legible viewing experience
          that adapts to any work environment, from a brightly lit front desk to a dimly lit
          back office in the middle of the night.
